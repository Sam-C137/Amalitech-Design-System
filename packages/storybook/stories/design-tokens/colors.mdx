# Colors
This is a collection of colors used in the design system. The colors are organized into different categories to make it as easy as possible for you to apply our color tokens, we use semantic names for the colors. If you change Amalitech components or develop new components, pay attention to the correct use of the color tokens. This is the only way to ensure that future updates can be easily transferred to your designs at the push of a button.


## Primary Colors
The primary colors for user interfaces are our brand colors. This makes the basic tonality of our websites and digital products friendly, open, and bright.
